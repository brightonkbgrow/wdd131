Fedex is a Domestic Shorthair with a tudexo pattern. He is nearly 2 years old and hasn't mentally aged since about 6 months.

He has olive green eyes and a spike of white fur that runs up the right side of his nose and past his eyes.


Fedex is a unique cat who take a lot of interest in anything that he isnt meant to have.
Fedex likes to play with and chew on all sorts of things! Such as:
DND Figurines
Trash Bags
Garlic Cloves
Facial Hair
Duct tape
